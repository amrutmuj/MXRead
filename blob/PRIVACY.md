# MXRead Privacy Policy

Last updated: [insert date]

MXRead is a mixed reality reading application for Meta Quest. It allows users to highlight text, write or draw annotations, place virtual sticky notes, and record voice notes linked to physical book pages.

## Data We Process or Store

MXRead may process and store the following data on the user’s device:

- Page-linked annotations, including highlights, drawings, written notes, and sticky notes.
- Voice notes recorded by the user inside the app.
- QR marker or page-tracking information used to associate annotations with book pages.
- Basic app state needed for the reading and annotation experience.

MXRead does not require users to create an account.

## How We Use Data

MXRead uses this data only to provide the app’s reading, annotation, note-taking, voice note, and page-linking features.

We do not sell user data. We do not use user data for advertising. We do not share user data with third parties.

## Microphone Use

MXRead uses microphone access only when the user chooses to record a voice note. Voice recordings are used to let the user replay notes linked to a specific page or reading session.

## Camera / Mixed Reality Use

MXRead uses Meta Quest mixed reality and tracking capabilities to support page-linked annotations and interaction with physical books. QR marker is used only for app functionality.

## Data Storage

Data is stored locally on the user’s device unless a future version clearly states otherwise. MXRead does not currently provide cloud sync or external account-based storage.

## Data Deletion

Users may delete their annotations, sticky notes, drawings, highlights, and voice notes from within the app where deletion controls are available.

Users may also delete all locally stored MXRead data by uninstalling the app from their Meta Quest device.

MXRead is a general-audience application and is not directed specifically to children under 13. Younger users should use MXRead only where permitted by the Meta Quest platform, their account settings, and applicable law.
